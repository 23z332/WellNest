Helpline for Women and Children in Coimbatore

Essential Contact Information

Below are the helplines and contact details for various services available to support women and children in Coimbatore. These organizations and authorities can provide guidance, assistance, and counseling in times of need.

Important Contacts:

  • Women’s Helpline (1091) – A national helpline providing immediate assistance to women in distress.
  • Childline (1098) – A 24/7 emergency helpline for children in need of care and protection.
  • Coimbatore Police Women’s Cell – 0422-2302145, offering support for women facing domestic violence or harassment.
  • Coimbatore District Child Protection Unit – 0422-2303566, providing services for child protection and welfare.
  • Psychological Counseling (SNEHA - Coimbatore) – 0422-2438023, offering mental health support for women and children.
  • National Commission for Women (NCW) – 011-26942369, a central body that addresses grievances related to women’s rights.
  • Coimbatore District Legal Services Authority – 0422-2315859, providing legal aid and services for women and children in need.
  • Women’s Helpline (Tamil Nadu - 181) – A dedicated helpline for women in Tamil Nadu offering immediate support.
  • Coimbatore Rape Crisis Center – 0422-2438231, offering support and guidance to victims of sexual violence.
  • State Child Protection Unit (Tamil Nadu) – 044-28521158, working for the welfare and protection of children across the state.

Other Useful Resources:

  • Self-Defense Classes for Women – Coimbatore offers various self-defense programs aimed at empowering women.
  • Women’s Empowerment Groups – Join local support groups for social welfare, empowerment, and safety.
  • Legal Aid Clinics – Free legal consultation for women and children facing legal issues.
  • Emergency Medical Assistance – In case of medical emergencies, contact Coimbatore General Hospital at 0422-2393700.
